Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about ONE Fit

Do I need equipment to start? +

No! One of the biggest advantages of calisthenics is that it requires zero equipment. You can start your journey using just your bodyweight. However, having a pull-up bar is beneficial for pull exercises, but it's optional.

How long does it take to see results? +

Most users report noticeable changes within 4-6 weeks. Significant transformation typically occurs within 8-12 weeks. Results depend on consistency, nutrition, and recovery. Strength gains appear faster than body composition changes.

Is ONE Fit suitable for beginners? +

Absolutely! Our beginner program is designed specifically for those starting their fitness journey. We provide detailed form guides, scaled variations, and progressive difficulty increases to ensure safe and sustainable progress.

Can women benefit from these programs? +

Yes! Calisthenics programs are excellent for women. They build lean muscle, increase strength, and can be adjusted for any fitness level. Our nutrition plans account for different metabolic needs and our community is inclusive and supportive.

How often should I train per week? +

Our beginner program recommends 3 days per week, intermediate 4 days, and advanced 5-6 days. Rest days are crucial for recovery and muscle growth. Listen to your body and adjust as needed. Quality over quantity!

Do you provide nutrition guidance? +

Yes! We provide three comprehensive nutrition plans: Muscle Gain, Fat Loss, and Maintenance. Each includes sample daily menus, macro breakdowns, and meal prep guides. Our nutrition recommendations complement your training program.

Is there a money-back guarantee? +

Yes! We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee with no questions asked. We're confident in our programs, and if you're not satisfied for any reason, you'll get a full refund. Your satisfaction is our priority.

Can I modify the programs? +

Absolutely! While our structured programs provide a proven framework, we encourage customization. Our progress tracker helps you modify intensity based on your recovery, goals, and preferences. Flexibility is key to long-term success.
